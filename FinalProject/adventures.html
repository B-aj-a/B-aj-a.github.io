Adventures

Open Season

This was the first adventure of the year which we start around February. We biked around the snowcapped glaciers of Glaciers National Park. Gear toward intermediate riders it was truly a blast to see the nature around us. This 20-mile ride is sure to get you heart pumping and we also include a small lunch at the end. Join us next year for $25 a person!

March Madness

Our second adventure starts in March. Just when spring start to hit and everything start to turn green. Our beginners riding adventure is bound to be a great event for the whole family. Join us on this 15-mile ride through some calm dirt trails just outside Bozeman MT. For just $25 a person and children under 10 and free, you’re bound to have a great time in nature riding with our experienced tour guides.
