Cycle the Continental Divide

Join us on our grand event, Cycle the Continental Divide! This is an experienced ride that will take all of the skill you have! Ride all 2768.4 miles of the trail starting in Banff, Alberta, Canada and end in Antelope Wells, New Mexico. Ranging in time frame from 6 to 10 weeks, make sure you pack all the gear you will need for a long ride. While most of the trail is paved, it has in challenge in its length, and its altitude changes. While this is a self-supported ride, we do offer guides starting at around $1,000. If this sounds like the challenge for you, be sure to sing up and we will send you more information!
